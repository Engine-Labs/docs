This is your team's email inbox. Use this view to monitor your teams email activity.

All inboxes have a @ctomail.io address. Custom domains are coming soon.

<Warning>
  Do not use or allow your agents to use the inbox in ways that violate our [acceptable use policy](https://docs.cto.new/acceptable-use-policy).

  This will result in inbox access being automatically paused and eventually permanently disabled.
</Warning>

You should carefully review any tasks that involve sending email. You must do this to prevent spam and other abusive actions that violate our [acceptable use policy](https://docs.cto.new/acceptable-use-policy).

Inbox health and usage is monitored automatically. Common actions that get flagged as abuse include:

- Agents guessing email addresses
- Sending emails with spammy content
- Sending non-compliant unsolicited emails
