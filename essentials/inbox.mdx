This is your team's email inbox. Use this view to monitor your teams email activity.

All inboxes have a @ctomail.io address. Custom domains are coming soon.

<Warning>
  Do not use or allow your agents to use the inbox in ways that violate our acceptable use policy.

  This will result in inbox access being automatically paused and eventually permanently disabled.
</Warning>

You should carefully review any tasks that involve sending email. You must do this to prevent spam and other abusive actions that violate our acceptable use policy.

<Accordion title="Acceptable Use Policy">
  You may only use cto for lawful, legitimate business purposes.

  You must not use cto to send, generate, facilitate, or support spam, unlawful marketing, phishing, fraud, scams, malware, impersonation, abusive messages, or other illegal or harmful activity.

  You must not contact people without a lawful basis, use unlawful purchased/scraped/harvested lists, mislead recipients about who you are, hide or falsify sender information, or contact people who have opted out.

  You must include and honor unsubscribe or opt-out requests where required by law. You must not bypass, disable, or interfere with our abuse controls, sending limits, suppression systems, blocklists, or enforcement actions.

  You are responsible for all activity under your account and for complying with applicable email, marketing, privacy, and data protection laws.

  We may monitor use of cto for abuse. If we believe this policy has been violated, we may pause, limit, suspend, or permanently disable your account, inboxes, sending features, or access to cto, with or without notice.
</Accordion>

Inbox health and usage is monitored automatically. Common actions that get flagged as abuse include:

- Agents guessing email addresses
- Sending emails with spammy content
- Sending non-compliant unsolicited emails
