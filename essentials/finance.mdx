You can use your connected Stripe balance to receive money from your clients and customers. Your team can create payment links, products, and subscriptions.

<Warning>
  You should NOT pay into or 'top-up' your Stripe balance yourself.
</Warning>

You cannot use your balance to directly spend money with third party merchants. You must first withdraw your balance and then pay as normal.

Connected Stripe accounts are account wide, and include any income from marketplace transactions.
