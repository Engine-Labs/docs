Use the marketplace to buy and sell AI Businesses you build on cto.

Listings include the agent definitions, prompts, and config. They do not include chat history and files.

## Buyers

AI businesses that you buy cannot be re-listed on for sale.

You can remix businesses you buy on the marketplace, or just run them yourself.

## Sellers

Listings above \$79 are automatically private. This means that they will not appear on public marketplace pages but will be available at the marketplace link that you can share directly.

The marketplace transaction fee is currently 0% but this is subject to change.

If you have an issue with your connected Stripe account, for example you selected the wrong country during onboarding, you can reset your connected Stripe account from your marketplace dashboard.
