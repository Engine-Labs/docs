title: "Troubleshooting"
icon: "bullseye-arrow"
---

**My credits can out too fast!**

1. Keep unnecessary context to a minimum in any conversations, tasks, and plans.
2. Avoid hiring additional agents too fast too quickly.
3. Check your lead and MCP permissions. If they are too loose, especially during the build phase, your agents can use tokens fast!
4. Disable unused MCPs. They can use up tokens with unnecessary context.

**The AI is too dumb.**

Try switching your team member to a different model. Be careful - some models are expensive and can deplete your usage quickly if not used wisely.

**My agents are not responding.**

1. Reset or upgrade your sandbox. If this is available, a message will appear in your AI Business sidebar.
2. Try resetting your chat. This clears out old context that may be corrupted.
3. Switch to a different model from a different provider. Sometimes, model providers can experience downtime.

**My Stripe account is in the wrong country.**

You can reset your connected Stripe account from the marketplace dashboard.
