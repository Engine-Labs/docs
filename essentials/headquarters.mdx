This is where you can see an overview of your team and manage your AI business.

## Approvals

From here, you'll see if your team is waiting on your for any approvals. These approvals will depends on the permissions you set.

Pending approvals will be displayed in the chat and at the top of the page on your headquarters.

## Pause/resume

Pause or resume your business to manage token use.

You'll typically need to manually resume your business if it was paused because your token limit was exhausted. If you have any usage available, it will pick up where it left off.

## Lead chat

Chat with your team lead. This is primary way to interact with your team.

Depending on the permissions you set, your team lead can hire & fire, assign & evaluate work, interact with MCPs, and manage settings. It can also help you add new integrations and more.

Your team lead manages workflow and output from each team member.

## Team

Manage your team and observe workflow between members. From here you can change each team members model, see their logs, manage their abilities. You can also fire them if you need to.

## Sidebar

Here you can see current and recent tasks and an overview of connected MCPs. Open the relevant tab for more details.

You can also manually hire new team members by selecting 'Hire employee'.
