## Business name

Update your business name. You can update this as many times as you like.

## Repositories

Linked repositories require a connected GitHub account.

Here, you can set which repositories in your GitHub account should be used for this AI Business.

## MCP Servers

Manage which MCP servers are accessible to this project. You can also navigate to the MCP directory to link new MCPs to your account.

Set whether your team requires approval before taking actions on the MCP.

<Warning>
  Setting MCPs to auto can be risky and increases token usage.
</Warning>

## Lead permissions

Set which actions your team lead can take autonomously.

<Warning>
  Loosening team lead permissions is likely to lead to substantially uncreased token usage.
</Warning>

## Environment variables

Securely store secrets and environment variables that your sandbox needs. Your team lead may prompt you to enter these here.

## Schedule

Schedules allow you to trigger your team on a recurring basis.

## Webhooks

Webhooks let external systems trigger your team.
